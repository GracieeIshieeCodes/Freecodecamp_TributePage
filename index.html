Leonardo da Vinci

The Renaissance Genius Who Transformed Art and Science

Here's a time line of Leonardo da Vinci's life:

  • 1452: Born on April 15 in Vinci, Italy.
  • 1466: Apprenticed to Andrea del Verrocchio, a prominent artist in Florence.
  • 1482: Moved to Milan, where he worked for Ludovico Sforza, Duke of Milan, as an engineer, artist, and architect.
  • 1495: Painted "The Last Supper" in the refectory of the Convent of Santa Maria delle Grazie in Milan.
  • 1503-1506: Created the famous portrait "Mona Lisa."
  • 1516: Moved to France under the patronage of King Francis I, where he continued his scientific studies until his death.
  • 1519: Passed away on May 2 in Amboise, France.
